1. The Goal

The goal of the game is to get three of your symbols in a row.

2. Turns

Player X starts first. After X plays, player O gets a turn.

3. How To Win

You can win with three symbols in a row, column, or diagonal.

4. Tie

If all places on the board are full and nobody wins, the game ends in a tie.
